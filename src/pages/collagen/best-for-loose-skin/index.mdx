# Best Collagen for Loose Skin After Weight Loss

Collagen supplementation has genuine clinical evidence for improving skin elasticity. But the category is full of products that under-dose, use inferior forms, or make claims the evidence doesn't support. Here's what actually matters when choosing a collagen supplement for loose skin.

*This content is for informational purposes only.*

---

## What the Evidence Shows

Multiple randomized controlled trials have shown that **hydrolyzed collagen peptides at 10g per day** improve skin elasticity, hydration, and collagen density over 8–12 weeks of consistent use.

The mechanism: hydrolyzed collagen is broken down into dipeptides and tripeptides — particularly proline-hydroxyproline (Pro-Hyp) — that are absorbed into the bloodstream and signal fibroblasts (the cells that produce collagen) to increase collagen synthesis. The effect is systemic, not limited to one area.

Key finding from the research: **dose matters**. Products dosed at 2.5–5g show more modest results than the 10g dose used in the strongest studies.

---

## What to Look For

**Hydrolyzed collagen peptides** — not gelatin, not whole collagen. Hydrolysis breaks the protein into smaller peptides that are absorbed more effectively. Look for "hydrolyzed collagen," "collagen peptides," or "collagen hydrolysate" on the label.

**10g per serving** — or the ability to reach 10g easily. Some products are dosed at 5g per scoop; you'd need two scoops. Products dosed at 1–2g are insufficient for loose skin.

**Bovine or marine source** — both have clinical evidence. Bovine (from cow hide) is more common and generally less expensive. Marine (from fish skin) is often cited for Type I collagen specifically, which is the primary collagen in skin. Either works.

**Minimal additives** — unflavored powders are the most versatile and typically the most cost-effective. Avoid products with excessive sugar, fillers, or proprietary blends that obscure the actual collagen dose.

---

## Adding Vitamin C

Collagen synthesis requires Vitamin C as a cofactor. Supplementing 500–1000mg of Vitamin C alongside collagen ensures the synthesis pathway isn't limited by deficiency — particularly relevant if your diet is inconsistent.

---

## How to Take It Consistently

The research benefit comes from **daily use over 8–12+ weeks**. The easiest way to maintain consistency:

- One scoop in morning coffee (unflavored powder dissolves completely and doesn't affect taste)
- Or in a morning smoothie
- Set it next to the coffee maker so the habit is automatic

Consistency matters more than timing. There's no meaningful difference between taking it in the morning vs. evening.

---

## What Collagen Won't Do

Collagen supplementation improves skin elasticity and supports the retraction process. It doesn't:
- Eliminate significant structural loose skin on its own
- Replace the effect of strength training, RF devices, or other active interventions
- Produce results in days or weeks — the timeline is 8–12 weeks minimum

It's one component of a multi-intervention protocol, not a standalone solution.
